    • About

    Pengfei Wang is an Associate Professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Dalian University of Technology, Dalian, China. His research interests are in the area of computer networking with emphasis on design, measurements, and prototype implementation of protocols and algorithms for the Internet and IoT.

    Dr. Wang joined the Dalian University of Technology after receiving a Ph.D. in Software Engineering from Northeastern University, Shenyang, China. From 2016 to 2018, he was a visiting Ph.D. student in Networks Group under the direction of Prof. Aleksandar Kuzmanovic at Northwestern University, Evanston, IL. He has published nearly 20 papers in high quality conferences and journals, such as IEEE INFOCOM, IEEE ICDCS, IEEE Global Internet Symposium, and IEEE Internet of Things Journal, etc. He is also served as a TPC member of ICCCN '21, HPCC '20, etc.
